## 🧭 Featured explainers

### [Orchestrated Distributed Intelligence (ODI) — a visual TLDR](/odi/)
A diagram-rich, plain-language explainer of the ODI paradigm: why the future of agentic AI lies not in smarter solo agents, but in **orchestrating many agents together with human judgment** into adaptive *systems of action*. Includes the evolution from Systems of Record → Automation → Agents → Action, the orchestration-layer architecture, and the three ingredients that make it work.

### [A New Way of Working — Agentic Consulting](/agentic-consulting/)
A three-part series on how agentic AI is reshaping the consulting operating model — rethinking delivery, talent, and the manager's role as a **systems orchestrator** of human and AI teams.
